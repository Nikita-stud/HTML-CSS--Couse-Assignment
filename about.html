Jerremy Willson

No One Will Get Cold

Our journey started in 1980 after Jerremy Willson travelled to the mountain Mormolade in Italy. On his way he got cold, his jacket did not protect him from the rain, nor the wet wind. After almost not surviving the trip, he has seen an opportunity to change the lives of a lot of people. During that time and age, no company has put themselves through the harsh weathers using their own jackets. This was about to change as Jerremy founded Rainydays.

-Now no one will get cold
